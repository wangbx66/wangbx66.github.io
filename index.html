Biography

I'm a sixth-year PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, under Prof. Siu On Chan. I also get advice from Prof. Andrej Bogdanov. My research interest lies on reinforcement learning, online learning, and Markov decesion process. I obtained my bachelor degree in Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2014. My major was cryptography and information security, under Prof. Hai Zhao.

I'm currently visiting University of Alberta and Borealis AI, Edmonton. I'm at room 3-232 CCIS building, University of Alberta.
