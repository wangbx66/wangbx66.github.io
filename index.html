Biography

I'm a sixth-year PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, under Prof. Siu On Chan. I also get supervision from Prof. Andrej Bogdanov. My research interest lies on reinforcement learning, including RL foundations, RL theory, trustworthy RL, and deep RL. Apart from RL I study interesting problems in machine learning, learning theory, and etc., as they arise.

I obtained my bachelor degree in Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2014. My major was Cryptography and Information Security, under Prof. Hai Zhao.
